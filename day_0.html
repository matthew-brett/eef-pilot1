Day 0¶

To start:

  • Get a study number from me;

  • Fill out this form;

  • Get a consent form from me, ask any questions, sign it.

  • Check your setup, by making a new Notebook:

    • Windows, with Anaconda - open Anaconda Prompt. Type jupyter notebook.
    • Mac, with Anaconda - open Terminal.app (maybe from Spotlight). Type jupyter notebook.

    Any problems, ask for help.
